About Book & Notes Vault

Book & Notes Vault is a personal library tracker built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Keep track of your books, tag them by category, and monitor your reading progress.

Features

  • Add, edit, and delete book records
  • Regex-powered live search with match highlighting
  • Sort by title, author, pages, or date
  • Dashboard with reading stats and 7-day trend
  • Monthly pages cap with live progress tracking
  • Reading time estimator
  • JSON import/export with validation
  • Full keyboard accessibility
  • Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
